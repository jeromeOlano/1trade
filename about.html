About Us

1 Trade Logistics, Inc.

1 Trade Logistics, Inc. (1Trade) is a duly registered corporation involved in the cargo transport business in the Philippines. The incorporators are trade compliance specialists and licensed Customs Brokers practicing their profession in imports, exports, transshipments, logistics and supply chain operations all over the Philippines

1Trade has shifted its focus on the weakest and most challenging link in the supply chain – FCL import trucking/delivery. 1Trade employed competent dispatchers, highly skilled helpers and experienced drivers to deliver service quality second to none. Since then, it has helped client importers in achieving “just-in-time” deliveries to their production lines, store doors, cross-dock facilities, and warehouses.
